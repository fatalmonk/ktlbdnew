## 🎯 Performance Indicators

### Green Light Indicators (On Track)

✅ Traffic growth >10% month-over-month  
✅ New keyword rankings trending up  
✅ Backlinks growing consistently  
✅ Content published on schedule  
✅ Technical issues resolved within 1 week  
✅ Conversion rate improving

### Yellow Light Indicators (Needs Attention)

⚠️ Traffic growth <10% month-over-month  
⚠️ Keyword rankings stagnant  
⚠️ Backlink growth slowing  
⚠️ Content behind schedule by 1-2 pieces  
⚠️ Technical issues unresolved for 1-2 weeks

### Red Light Indicators (Urgent Action)

🚨 Traffic declining month-over-month  
🚨 Keyword rankings dropping significantly  
🚨 Lost important backlinks  
🚨 Content severely behind schedule (3+ pieces)  
🚨 Critical technical issues (site down, major errors)  
🚨 Conversion rate declining
