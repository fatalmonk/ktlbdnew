## 📝 How to Update Your Google Business Profile

1. Go to [Google Business Profile Manager](https://business.google.com/)
2. Select the profile you want to edit
3. Click on "Edit profile" → "Business information"
4. Scroll to "Business description"
5. Click "Edit" and paste your optimized description
6. Ensure all 5 keywords are naturally included
7. Click "Save"
8. Repeat for the second profile
