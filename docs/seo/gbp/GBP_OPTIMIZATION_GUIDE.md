#### Q2: Where is Kattali Textile Limited located?

**A:** Kattali Textile Limited has two locations:

- **Head Office:** BM Heights, 8th Floor, 318 Sk. Mujib Road, Agrabad, Chittagong, Bangladesh
- **Production Facility:** North Kattali Industrial Area, Chittagong, Bangladesh

We're a textile exporter Chittagong serving global markets from our Bangladesh-based facilities.

**Keywords:** Textile exporter Chittagong, Bangladesh

#### Q3: What is your minimum order quantity (MOQ)?

**A:** Our minimum order quantity is typically 5,000 units per style. However, MOQ may vary depending on the product type and customization requirements. Contact our sales team at sales@ktlbd.com or +8801734855403 for specific MOQ details and custom pricing.

**Keywords:** MOQ, woven garment supplier

---

#### Q4: Do you have certifications for sustainable manufacturing?

**A:** Yes! Kattali Textile Limited is committed to sustainable textile production and holds multiple certifications including:

- Accord Fire & Building Safety
- Sedex Member (ethical sourcing)
- Nirapon Certified
- ISO certified

We follow eco-friendly practices and ethical manufacturing standards in all our operations.

**Keywords:** Sustainable textile production, certifications

---

#### Q5: What markets do you serve?

**A:** As a premier textile exporter Chittagong, we serve global markets including North America, Europe, and the Middle East. We work with international fashion brands and retailers, delivering high-quality garments manufactured in our Bangladesh facilities.

**Keywords:** Textile exporter Chittagong, global markets

---

#### Q6: Do you manufacture denim products?

**A:** Yes! Kattali Textile Limited is a leading denim manufacturer Bangladesh, producing premium denim jeans, jackets, and denim apparel. Our North Kattali facility specializes in denim production with eco-friendly washing processes and quality assurance. Contact us for denim product inquiries.

**Keywords:** Denim manufacturer Bangladesh

---

#### Q7: Can you handle custom designs?

**A:** Absolutely! As a full-service woven garment supplier, we offer custom design services. Our team works with clients from concept to production, including design development, sample creation, and mass production. Contact our design team to discuss your custom requirements.

**Keywords:** Woven garment supplier, custom designs

---

#### Q8: What are your business hours?

**A:** Our business hours are:

- Monday - Friday: 9:00 AM - 6:00 PM (Bangladesh Standard Time)
- Saturday: 9:00 AM - 2:00 PM
- Sunday: Closed

For international clients, we're available via email 24/7 and respond within 24 hours.
